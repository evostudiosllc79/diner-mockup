Our Story

Vintage Restaurant Serving Delight in Mesa, AZ

Dan's Diner is all about good food and good times. Step in and be transported to the nostalgic era of the 50s with our unique and inviting vibe. Whether you're in the mood for a hearty breakfast, a satisfying lunch, or a delectable dinner, we have you covered. Located in the heart of Mesa, our neon-lit and chrome building is an iconic landmark that simply cannot be missed. So why wait?
