Our Story

A Mesa landmark built on good food, warm hospitality, and a whole lot of heart.

Who We Are

Where Every Meal Feels Like Home

Step into our throwback 1950s diner, located in the heart of Mesa, Arizona, where we proudly serve American staples for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. As a family-owned business, we have a simple yet powerful mission: to dish up great food at a great price. Our love for our regulars is unmatched, and we strive to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere that makes everyone feel like part of our family.

The Atmosphere

Step Into the 50s

Chrome counters. Red vinyl booths. Neon signs glowing warm against the Mesa sky. A jukebox spinning classic hits in the corner. Nana Dee's isn't just a place to eat — it's an experience.

Whether you're catching breakfast before work, bringing the family for a weekend treat, or just craving that perfect milkshake, the moment you walk through our door, you're transported somewhere a little warmer, a little simpler, and a whole lot more delicious.
